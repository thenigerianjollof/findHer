ABOUT

We are findHer. A tech-inspired business by women for women. Our product is a button created to help women who feel unsafe send their location to family members or firends(called "holders") to help assist. The way the product works is when a woman presses the button andit will send an email to her location holder sending them your coordinates. We must include that while our product was created as an answer to the high state of unsafety experienced by women, it can also cater to men.

OUR FUTURE

As our company expands, our main focus which is to keep women safe remains a top priority. Making it easier to use each account to send text messages or email to user holders. Changing our current arduino model to a poratable keychain button.
