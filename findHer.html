ABOUT

We are findHer. an all women business interested in helping other women feel safe. Our product was invented to help women who feel unsafe send their location to family members to help assist. The way the product works is when a woman presses the button on her keychain it will send an email to your location holder sending them your coordinates.

OUR FUTURE

As our company expands, our main focus which is to keep women safe remains a top priority. We have exciting considerations for our current findHer model.These include: Connecting our webpage to a database that collects user accounts information. making it easier to use each account and customize holders. Changing our model to a poratable keychain.We also have an evolving five year plan that allows for investors and more collaborators for the business as a whole unit.

OUR PARTNERS

Our partners include a non-profit busines focused on keeping women safe. We plan to keep pushing for the safety of women and girls in compromising and uncomfortable situations.
